# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

> An end-to-end Data Analysis project using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to uncover customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, and business insights from retail transaction data.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional retail data containing **3,900 customer purchases** across multiple product categories.

The objective of this project is to identify:
- Customer spending patterns
- Product preferences
- Revenue-driving factors
- Subscription behavior
- Discount impact
- Customer loyalty trends

The project combines:
- **Python** for data cleaning and preprocessing
- **SQL (MySQL)** for business analysis
- **Power BI** for interactive dashboard visualization

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

A retail company wanted to better understand customer shopping behavior in order to:

- Improve customer engagement
- Increase customer retention
- Optimize product strategy
- Enhance marketing campaigns
- Increase subscription conversions
- Improve overall sales performance

The company noticed changing purchasing patterns across:
- Demographics
- Product categories
- Discounts
- Shipping preferences
- Subscription status

The goal was to leverage customer data to generate actionable business insights.

# 📈 Key Insights

## 👥 Customer Insights
- Loyal customers formed the majority of customers
- Young adults generated the highest revenue
- Most customers were non-subscribers

---

## 🛍️ Product Insights
- Clothing category had the highest sales
- Gloves received the highest average rating
- Some products were highly discount dependent

---

## 💰 Revenue Insights
- Male customers generated higher revenue
- Express shipping customers spent slightly more
